About me

Hello, I’m Qingjun Chang. I studied at Ningbo Institute of Technology, Zhejiang University (ZJUNIT) from September 2013 to June 2017. I received my B.S. degree in Information and Computing Science from ZJUNIT in 2017. In my undergraduate stage, with the supervision and help of Prof. Zhenyu Shu, I began to contact and study some aspects of Digital Geometry Processing and Machine Learning.

I received the M.S. degree in Mathematics from Hangzhou Dianzi University (HDU) in 2020, under the supervision of Prof. Chongyang Deng. I am interested in Computer Aided Geometric Design & Computer Graphics. During this period, my main topic was Generalized Barycentric Coordinates (GBC).

I have studied and visited at the Università della Svizzera italiana (USI) in Switzerland, under the supervision of Prof. Kai Hormann, from September to December 2019.

From April 2020 to September 2020, I worked at the Southeast Digital Economic Development Institute (SDEDI) as a researcher in the 3D Digital Space R&D Center. During this period, my main research topics include 3D Building Change Detection and 3D Scene Data Compression.

Since March 2021, I am a PhD student at the Università della Svizzera italiana (USI) in Switzerland, funded by the Swiss National Science Foundation (SNSF),and working on the porject “Barycentric Interpolation“ with Prof. Kai Hormann.
